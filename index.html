Eternitas Games
We're creating browser games that are free, open source, always online, and where players truly own their data.
Our mission
We create sufficiently decentralized games that run entirely on the blockchain by leveraging TelosZero smart contracts as the game server, and utilizing TelosEVM to securely store player tokens and collectibles in their Metamask wallets. Thanks to the continuous operation of Telos without downtime, our games are always online, guaranteeing seamless accessibility. Although the use of Metamask is optional, players who opt in stand to earn token rewards, providing an additional incentive for active and skilled players.
Our games
States of Matter
Turn based PvP card game
GAMEPLAY
There are five card classes and four card types in States of Matter, each with its own unique set of utilities. Build a deck that suits your playstyle and duel opponents in one of the three game modes.
FREE TO PLAY
All the cards in the game will always be free to play. No more hiding meta decks behind paywalls, and no predatory pay-to-win monetization forcing you to spend more just to keep up.
PLAY TO EARN
You are rewarded with tokens for maintaining a top position on the leaderboards, achieving high rankings, completing tasks, and leveling up. You can then spend those tokens to craft various cosmetics for your cards and profile.
